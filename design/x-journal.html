"x journal is an annual, visual arts publication created in 2017. x is proud to publish, share, and archive the work made by students at knox college."

Behind the Design

For this x journal, the design direction centered on the theme "liminal". As the designer and artwork photographer, I helped direct the aesthetic vision and thematic development, establishing a cohesive design language.

Capturing the Work

A significant portion of this issue's identity relied on the high-fidelity reproduction of physical art. I was responsible for photographing, color-correcting, and cataloging every piece of student artwork. By balancing clean interior layouts, the design ensured that the art remained the focal point while the typography provided clean guiding structure.

Campus Presence & Production

Extending the journal's reach required a visual strategy that lived outside the book. I created a series of interactive promotional visuals designed to spark engagement and encourage submissions across campus.

This holistic approach concluded with a close collaboration with professional printers—managing press checks, layout adjustments, and proofreading to ensure the final physical object met rigorous design standards.

Together, the journal stands as a record of collective student creativity, refined through a lens of professional technical execution and a commitment to maintaining the integrity of the original artwork.
