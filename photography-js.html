Gai Jatra

Gai Jatra is a festival celebrated by the Newari people of Kathmandu Valley. The festival is celebrated in honour of their immediate relatives who have died during the previous year.

People, mostly children, who don't fully understand the concept of death, dress up as in religious drags and mourn the death of their loved ones.
